## Article Structure

### 1. Hook (150 words)
- Problem: Content creation is exhausting
- Stat: Average YouTuber spends 40+ hours per video
- Promise: AI cuts this to 10-15 hours
- Personal angle: "I tested 30+ AI tools"

### 2. Quick Comparison Table
- Top 10 tools, sortable by category, price, platform
- Star ratings, best-for tags

### 3. How I Tested (E-E-A-T)
- Created 20 pieces of content across platforms
- Timed each workflow step
- Compared output quality
- Tested free vs paid tiers

### 4. Category Deep-Dives

- What it does (1 sentence)
- Who it's for (specific creator type)

- Weaknesses (2-3 bullets, honest)
- Pricing (exact numbers)
- Real example from testing
