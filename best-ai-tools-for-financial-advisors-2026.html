Regulatory & Compliance Considerations

FINRA/SEC Requirements: - ✅ All marketing content must be pre-approved - ✅ Social media posts require supervision - ✅ AI recommendations need human oversight - ✅ Client communications must be archived - ✅ Performance reporting must be accurate

Choosing Compliant Tools: 1. Check for built-in compliance features (approval workflows, archiving) 2. Verify FINRA/SEC integration (required for broker-dealers) 3. Ensure audit trails (all AI recommendations logged) 4. Confirm data security (SOC 2 Type II minimum) 5. Review vendor disclaimers (who's liable if AI makes an error?)

Red Flags: ❌ No compliance review workflow
❌ Can't export data for audits
❌ Unclear about AI training data sources
❌ No human-in-the-loop safeguards

Common Implementation Mistakes

  1. Too Many Tools at Once: Start with CRM + planning software. Master those. Then add.

  2. No Training Budget: AI tools require 10-20 hours training per person. Budget for it.

  3. Skipping Data Migration: Bad data in = bad AI outputs. Clean your data first.

  4. Ignoring Compliance: Get your CCO involved DAY ONE. Compliance failure = career-ending.

  5. No Integration Plan: Siloed tools waste time. Choose tools that integrate.

  6. Set-and-Forget: AI improves with feedback. Review outputs weekly.
