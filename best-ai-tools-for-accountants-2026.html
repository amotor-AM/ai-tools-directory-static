Implementation Roadmap

Month 1: Assessment & Planning

  • Audit current processes
  • Identify biggest time sinks (data entry? reconciliation? close?)
  • Define success metrics (hours saved, error rate, close time)
  • Budget allocation

Month 2: Core Platform

  • Implement primary accounting system (Xero/QuickBooks/Intacct)
  • Migrate chart of accounts
  • Set up bank feeds
  • Train team (budget 20-40 hours)

Month 3: Automation Layer

  • Add receipt/invoice processing (Dext or Stampli)
  • Configure rules and workflows
  • Test accuracy (aim for 95%+)

Month 4: Advanced Tools

  • Close management (FloQast or BlackLine)
  • Practice management (Karbon)
  • Specialty tools (MindBridge, Trullion)

Month 5: Optimization

  • Review metrics vs. baseline
  • Tune AI models (categorization rules, approval workflows)
  • Train additional team members
  • Document new processes

Month 6: Scale

  • Expand to additional entities/clients
  • Add integrations
  • Consider custom AI models (Nanonets)

Common Implementation Mistakes

  1. Skipping Process Documentation: Document current state BEFORE implementing AI. You need a baseline.

  2. Over-Automation Too Fast: Start with ONE high-impact area (e.g., receipt processing). Master it. Then expand.

  3. Insufficient Training: AI tools require 10-20 hours of training. Budget for it.

  4. No Accuracy Validation: Check AI outputs manually for the first 30-60 days. Verify accuracy before going hands-off.

  5. Ignoring Integration: Tools that don't integrate = data silos. Choose tools with strong APIs.

  6. No Change Management: Your team may resist. Involve them early. Show time savings, not job replacement.
